About

I'm currently on a planned career break, using the time to explore, build, and figure out what comes next. This site is where I share what I make.

Before this, I spent two years at Accenture Strategy & Consulting as an Associate Consultant. My biggest project was a $400M regulatory remediation programme for a large UK investment bank — I ran governance across five workstreams and 100+ stakeholders, wrote PRDs translating regulatory mandates into tech specs, owned the product roadmap for a reporting platform, and designed a change management system from scratch.

Outside of that engagement, I worked on talent strategy for a leading Japanese bank — benchmarking global wealth management firms and helping operationalise training at scale.

Earlier, I got to do some interesting research. I was selected for the MITACS Globalink Research Fellowship at the University of Calgary, working on transportation equity and spatial data analysis. I also received the SURGE Research Grant at IIT Kanpur (top 2% of 2,000+ applicants), where I worked on visualising LSTM neural networks for traffic state prediction.

I graduated from IIT Kanpur with a B.Tech in Civil Engineering (GPA 8.2/10). Engineering taught me to think in systems. I ended up in product — turns out those skills travel.

When I'm not building, I read. I managed the Book Club at IIT Kanpur, overseeing 2,000+ books. Figuring out what to read next is an ongoing obsession, which is part of why Reading Companion was the first thing I built.
